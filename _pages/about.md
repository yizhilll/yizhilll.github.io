### Research

My current research focus involves:

- Building generalizable and scalable agents (<a href="https://github.com/IQuestLab/IQuest-Coder-V1">IQuest-Coder-V1</a>).
- Reinforcement Learning for LLM (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.17445">TreePO</a>).
- Latent & looped models.

Before that, some of my research interests involve: 

- Synthetic Data for Vision-Language Model (<a href="https://aclanthology.org/2025.acl-long.680/">MAmmoTH-VL</a>), 
- LM Evaluation (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24867">Encyclo-K</a>, <a href="https://neurips.cc/virtual/2025/loc/san-diego/poster/121825/">SuperGPQA</a>)
- Music modelling, built the universal understanding (<a href="https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/file/33dffa2e3d2ab74a783d1a8c292f66d9-Paper-Conference.pdf">MERT</a>) and generative model (<a href="https://map-yue.github.io/">YuE</a>) for music .

More recent and detailed topics can be referred to my <a href="https://yizhilll.github.io/publications/">publication pages</a>.
